About Bus Muafakat Johor

Bus Muafakat Johor is a bus service provided by the Johor State Government through 4 Local Authorities and operated by the Johor Public Transportation Corporation (PAJ). The purpose of this service is to reduce the cost of living of citizens and to provide facilities and also to improve the quality of public transport services to the people in the identified routes.

This service provided were free of charge to all citizens. Bus Muafakat Johor service route consists of 15 routes in the Iskandar Malaysia region covering Johor Bahru which include Johor Bahru, Pasir Gudang and Kulai areas. A total of 30 buses will be equipped with CCTV System, GPS, Passenger Data System and Free Internet Facility (WIFI)

In order to get Bus Muafakat Johor Service, users need to register and get the Muafakat Johor Card from the provided counter near them.

Muafakat Johor Card

About Muafakat Johor Card

Muafakat Johor Card is a card that were specially prepared for Bus Muafakat Johor users to enable them to board the bus for free. Card annual minimum charge will apply.This card can't be sold to others. The use of this card will be enhanced in the future which will serve as an electronic card for cashless transactions.

Passengers were required to show and touched the card on the sensors at the provided places whenever they take a bus and get off the bus. Failure to exhibit and touch the Muafakat Johor Card will cause passengers not to have this free bus service.

Muafakat Johor Card Registration

For card registrations, user were required to fill out the details of personal information. Such information will be managed in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010. Any information filled or collected is subject to PAJ's privacy policy. PAJ reserves the right to make any changes to the privacy policy. For registration, you can obtain the card at the following places given. Please check the PAJ official website from time to time.

How To Use

How To Use Bus Muafakat Johor Card

Every user should always ensure that the card is kept neatly. For any event of a loss or theft of a card, you may report the loss of the card and obtain a new card at the PAJ office. An annual minimum of each card charge will apply. If you have any questions about Muafakat Johor Cards, you can email your inquiries to this link below.
