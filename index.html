Bus Muafakat Johor

“Bus Muafakat Johor” is a services that were provided by the state government of Johor. This services is an effort that were made in order to build a systematic transportation in Johor Bahru. This services were mainly being provided toward the residential of Johor as well to improve the amenities or the infrastructure of the public bus itself.

Operated By

This bus servces were operated by an organzation which operate all the public transport in Johor Bahru. This organization known as "Perbadanan Pengangkutan Awam Johor" (PAJ). This services were operated for 4 local authorities which include MPJBT,MBJB,MPKu & MPPG

F.O.C

The service provided were free of charge for all citizen. User only need to register Muafakat Johor Card in order to get Bus Muafakat Johor services

Facilities

Consist of 15 routes covering Iskandar region include Johor Bahru, Kulai and Kulai. Providing 30 buses which equipped with CCTV System, GPS and also Passenger Data System and Free Internet Facility. Also provided user a system to ease user in searching their location and needed bus.

Get The Bus

Select Bus Muafakat Johor as a choice to ride > applying the "Bus Muafakat Johor Route Network System" for searching the bus info > show and touch "Muafakat Johor Card" on the sensor whenever take anf get off the bus > having a nice ride with Bus Muafakat Johor services

Registration

Registration for Muafakat Johor Card required user to fill out their details of personal information. For the registration and obtaining the card, user may get those on the nearby provided counter
